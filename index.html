Exhibitions

"Artistas Emergentes" Exhibition

July, 2022

Sevilla, España

This event provided an interesting platform for cultural expression and appreciation. It involved emerging artists from different countries exposing original pieces that promoted contemporary and social issues. Argentina, Chile, Colombia, El Salvador, España, Indonesia, México, Perú, Portugal, Uruguay and Venezuela, were represented by creative artists. Each of these places originality and transcendence were found at the galery in the South of Spain.

Plastic Arts Salon Exhibition

September, 2022

Montevideo, Uruguay

The exhibition gathered expressions and appreciations of diverse artistic perspectives. The theme ranged from nature to personal narratives and the different art styles, including paintings, sculptures, photographs, contributed to the richness of the exhibition. Artists succeed in showing the country´s principal landscapes, flora, fauna and cultural elements with diverse interpretations and styles. Were three really enriching days for the art community and cultural landscape.
